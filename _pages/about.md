Hi!

My name is **Wu Feiyang (吴飞洋)**. I am currently a Research Assistant at the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), and I have received a PhD offer from the same institution. My advisor is Prof. Zhaoyu Zhang. My research interests are: **Micro/Nano Optics, Semiconductor Devices, Optoelectronic Devices, Photonic Chips**.

# Education

- 2021.09 -- 2025.06, B.S. in Physics, Taishan College, Shandong University
- 2025.08 -- Present, Research Assistant (PhD offer received), School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), supervised by Prof. Zhaoyu Zhang

# Research Highlights

- **GaAs Photonic Crystal Surface-Emitting Laser (PCSEL)**: Full 3D electromagnetic simulation using Lumerical FDTD and COMSOL; automated simulation workflow via Claude Code agent and Java/Python API; involved in fabrication, characterization, and process management.
- **Thin-Film Lithium Niobate / Tantalum Niobate Waveguides**: Mode analysis and phase matching simulation using COMSOL FEM; studied broadband nonlinear frequency conversion in integrated photonic devices.
- **Memristor-based Reservoir Computing**: Multimodal signal processing with dynamic memristors for neuromorphic computing (co-author on two published papers).
